Product Maintenance App
(for Lee Lee)

The project started as an experiment, but due to the robust and customaizeable of self-managed application, it provided the value to my employer. Thus, the I was able to convinvced them to switch thier product managament application to this project. It have been evolved ever since the genesis initiatetion, the project is still being maintain by me until now.
Functionality provided: Manage and update products. Allow products search (provide both employee view and customer view). Compatible with Zebra and Dymo label printer. View daily sale report.
Key feature: Customizable. Open-source.
